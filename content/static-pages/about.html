About Sitruna

Sitruna is a comprehensive knowledge management platform designed to help organizations organize, connect, and extract value from their information assets.

Our mission is to transform fragmented information into connected knowledge that drives innovation and decision-making.

Key Features

Knowledge Graph

At the core of Sitruna is a powerful knowledge graph that maps relationships between concepts, documents, people, and projects. This graph-based approach enables powerful discovery and insights that traditional systems cannot provide.

Smart Content Management

Sitruna automatically organizes and tags content using advanced AI techniques, making it easier to find and use information when needed.

Collaboration Tools

Built-in collaboration features allow teams to work together effectively around knowledge assets, including commenting, sharing, and collaborative editing.

Integration Capabilities

Sitruna connects with your existing tools and systems, ensuring that knowledge flows seamlessly throughout your organization's technology ecosystem.
